GET OUT BP

Our demands.

Ending research ties between Princeton and British Petroleum by not renewing the contract between the Carbon Mitigation Initiative and BP.

Replacing BP funding for CMI and other Princeton climate research with an expanded Energy Research Fund to protect academic freedom.

Democratizing the University decision-making process around divestment and dissociation to include student voices by default.
